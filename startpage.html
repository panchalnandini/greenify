♻ Recycling Challenge

Sort and recycle household items correctly to earn points and reduce waste.

Beginner Challenge ⏱ 15 min 🏆 +50 points

📋 Instructions

  1. Collect all recyclable items from your home.
  2. Separate them into categories: plastic, glass, paper, metal.
  3. Dispose of them in the correct recycling bins.
  4. Take a photo as proof and upload it.
